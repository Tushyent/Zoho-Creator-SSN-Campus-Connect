## 📌 Overview
SSN Campus Connect is an AI-powered web application developed using Zoho Creator to connect students, seniors, faculty, and alumni within a unified platform. The system is designed to improve communication, streamline access to academic resources, and provide timely information about opportunities such as internships, hackathons, and placements.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
In a typical college environment, students often face challenges such as lack of proper guidance from seniors and alumni, missing out on important opportunities like hackathons and internships, and the absence of a centralized platform for study materials and academic discussions. Additionally, resolving doubts and reporting issues is often unstructured and inefficient. These gaps lead to reduced awareness, missed growth opportunities, and limited collaboration among students.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
SSN Campus Connect addresses these challenges by providing a centralized, AI-enabled platform where students can interact, share knowledge, and stay updated with relevant opportunities. The application enables seamless communication between juniors, seniors, faculty, and alumni, while also offering intelligent recommendations using Zia AI to enhance user engagement and decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Features
- 💬 Smart Doubt Discussion Forum with optional anonymity  
- 📢 Events & Opportunities Board (hackathons, internships, jobs)  
- 📚 Study Resource Repository (notes, question papers, roadmaps)  
- 🆘 Helpdesk System for reporting and tracking issues  
- 🤝 Alumni Referral & Career Support System  
- 🤖 AI-based Recommendations using Zia  
- 🔔 Automated Notifications and Alerts
